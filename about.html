OUR STORY


At Shroom Technologies, we are developing a 100% Bio-based and home compostable packaging material from mycelium. Our technology involves the use of natural and biodegradable raw materials as the substrate to grow mycelium into a required shape. A mould of interest is to be designed as per the requirement of a packaging body, which is to be filled with the substrate and the mycelium mixed together and let it grow. The product is then dried at to disable further growth of mycelium. The packaging material thus obtained will be tested for its physical characteristics like strength, elasticity, flexibility, thermal conductivity, moisture storage etc. So, these packaging materials can replace the use of plastics and as well as paper for real!
